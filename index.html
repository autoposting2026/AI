Requirements

Before we can begin automating your content, please ensure the following requirements are met:

  • You must have a registered account on the platform(s) where you want to post.
  • You must have an OpenAI API account with a minimum balance of $5.
  • For Facebook and Instagram, a business account is required, and both must be connected through Meta.
  • We will need access to each platform you want us to work with. Note: You may occasionally need to confirm logins via your phone, so please keep this in mind once we begin.
  • For a WordPress blog, we will need administrator access, and an additional plugin must be installed to allow communication with the automation scenario.
